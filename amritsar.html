Discover the Spirituality of Amritsar

Amritsar, a city steeped in history and spirituality, is home to some of the most iconic landmarks in India. Here are some must-visit places in this vibrant city:

Golden Temple

Visit the holiest shrine of Sikhism, the Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib). The shimmering gold facade is reflected in the sacred Amrit Sarovar, creating a serene atmosphere. Partake in the Langar (community kitchen) and experience the warmth of Sikh hospitality.

Jallianwala Bagh

Reflect on the poignant history of Jallianwala Bagh, the site of the infamous massacre in 1919. The memorial garden stands as a reminder of the sacrifices made during the struggle for independence, with bullet marks still visible on the walls.

Wagah Border

Experience the patriotic fervor at Wagah Border, where the Beating Retreat ceremony takes place daily. The synchronized lowering of the flags by the Indian and Pakistani border guards is a captivating display of national pride.

Plan your visit to Amritsar to soak in the spirituality, history, and cultural richness that defines this vibrant city.
