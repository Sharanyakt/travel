Discover the Rich Heritage of Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a mesmerizing destination in Karnataka, India, known for its ancient ruins and rich history. Here are some of the must-visit places in Hampi:

Virupaksha Temple

Begin your journey at the Virupaksha Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva. This ancient temple is the heart of Hampi and a living testament to the region's vibrant cultural and religious heritage.

Hampi Bazaar

Stroll through the enchanting Hampi Bazaar, a lively street flanked by ancient structures and vibrant market stalls. Immerse yourself in the local atmosphere and explore the unique handicrafts and artifacts.

Vijaya Vittala Temple

Visit the magnificent Vijaya Vittala Temple, renowned for its iconic stone chariot and musical pillars. The intricate architecture and historical significance make this temple a highlight of Hampi's cultural heritage.

Embark on a journey through time as you explore the ruins and marvel at the architectural wonders that Hampi has to offer.
