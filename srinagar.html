Discover the Beauty of Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is a paradise on earth with its breathtaking landscapes and serene lakes. Here are some of the must-visit places in this enchanting city:

Dal Lake

Embark on a shikara ride on Dal Lake, a picturesque water body surrounded by the Himalayas. Experience the floating gardens, vibrant houseboats, and the Mughal gardens along the shores, creating a serene and unforgettable atmosphere.

Shalimar Bagh

Visit the Shalimar Bagh, a Mughal garden built by Emperor Jahangir. Admire the terraced lawns, vibrant flowerbeds, and the charming water channels. This garden is a perfect retreat to experience the Mughal influence on the region.

Jama Masjid

Explore the historic Jama Masjid, a splendid example of Persian architecture, located in the heart of Srinagar. The grandeur of its wooden architecture and the peaceful courtyard make it a significant cultural and religious site.

Plan your visit to Srinagar to immerse yourself in the tranquility and beauty that defines this gem in the Himalayas.
