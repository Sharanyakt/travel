Discover the Grandeur of India Gate

India Gate, a symbol of national pride and a tribute to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives, is a must-visit landmark in New Delhi. Here are some key features and places around India Gate:

India Gate Monument

Standing tall in the heart of Delhi, India Gate is a war memorial dedicated to the Indian soldiers who died in World War I. The iconic structure, inspired by the Arc de Triomphe in Paris, is beautifully illuminated at night, creating a spectacular sight.

Rajpath

Take a stroll along Rajpath, the ceremonial boulevard that connects India Gate with the Rashtrapati Bhavan. Lined with lush green lawns and impressive fountains, Rajpath offers a picturesque view of the surrounding architecture and landscaping.

National War Memorial

Adjacent to India Gate, the National War Memorial is dedicated to honoring the armed forces' sacrifices. Explore the various sections of the memorial, each commemorating different aspects of India's military history.

Visit India Gate to experience the blend of history, architecture, and patriotism that this iconic landmark offers.
