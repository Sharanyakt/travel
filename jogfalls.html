Discover the Majestic Jog Falls

Jog Falls, located in the Shimoga district of Karnataka, is one of the highest plunge waterfalls in India. The sheer beauty and raw power of Jog Falls make it a must-visit destination. Here are some key attractions around Jog Falls:

Jog Falls Viewpoint

Begin your journey at the Jog Falls viewpoint to witness the breathtaking sight of the water cascading down the rugged cliffs. The viewpoint provides a panoramic view, allowing you to appreciate the grandeur of this natural wonder.

Linganamakki Dam

Explore the nearby Linganamakki Dam, the source of the Sharavathi River. The dam not only contributes to the hydroelectric power generation but also offers a serene environment surrounded by lush greenery, providing an ideal spot for a peaceful retreat.

Kanoor Fort

Visit the historical Kanoor Fort, which stands as a testament to the region's rich heritage. The fort provides a glimpse into the past, and the surrounding landscape offers a picturesque backdrop for history enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Plan your visit to Jog Falls to marvel at the natural beauty, experience the local history, and enjoy the tranquility of the surrounding landscapes.
